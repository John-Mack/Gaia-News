Server Commands

Essential Commands

/spawn

Teleport to the server spawn point

/home [name]

Teleport to your home location

/sethome [name]

Set a home at your current location

/delhome [name]

Delete a home location

/rtp

Random teleport to a safe location

/kit

List all available kits

/disposal

Open disposal GUI to delete items

Communication Commands

/msg [player] [message]

Send a private message to a player

/r [message]

Reply to the last private message

/mail

Open your mailbox

/mail send [player] [message]

Send mail to another player

/ignore [player]

Ignore a player's messages

/list

Show all online players

Teleport Requests (TPA)

/tpa [player]

Request to teleport to a player

/tpahere [player]

Request a player to teleport to you

/tpaaccept

Accept a teleport request

/tpadeny

Deny a teleport request

Land Claims (SimpleClaims)

/sc

Open the claiming party GUI

/sc claim

Claim the chunk you're standing on

/claim

Open claim interface

Economy Commands

/pay [player] [amount]

Send money to another player

/bank

Access your bank (EcotaleCoins)

/bank deposit all

Deposit all coins into your bank

/bank withdraw all

Withdraw all coins from your bank

Jobs System (EcotaleJobs)

/jobs

Open the jobs menu

/jobs browse

Browse available jobs

/jobs join [job]

Join a job

/jobs leave [job]

Leave a job

/jobs info [job]

View job information

Market Commands (EcotaleShops)

/market

Open the player market

/market browse

Browse market listings

/market sell

List items for sale

/market profile

View your market profile

/market claim

Claim earnings from sales

Admin Shop Commands

/adminshop

Show admin shop help

/adminshop buy [item]

Buy items from the admin shop

/adminshop sell

RPG Leveling (EndlessLeveling)

/el

Open the main EndlessLeveling menu

/skills

Open skill attributes GUI to allocate points

/el stats

View your character stats and progression

/el leaderboard

View ranked player progression leaderboard

/el settings

Open settings GUI to configure preferences

/el race

Choose or change your character race

/el class

Choose or change your character class

/el augments

View and manage your augments

/el passives

View your passive abilities and bonuses

/el party create

Create a party for group play

/el party invite [player]

Invite a player to your party

/el party disband

Disband your current party (leader only)

Utility Commands

/rules

Display server rules

/discord

Get Discord invite link

/motd

Show message of the day

/afk

Toggle AFK status

/playtime

Check your playtime

Administrator Commands

/kick [player]

Kick a player from the server

/mute [player] [time]

Mute a player for specified duration

/tempban [player] [time]

Temporarily ban a player

/ipban [player]

Permanently ban a player's IP

/god [player]

Toggle god mode (invincibility)

/heal [player]

Heal a player to full health

/fly [player]

Toggle fly mode

/vanish

Toggle invisibility mode

/freeze [player]

Freeze a player in place

/broadcast [message]

Send a server-wide broadcast

Note: Some commands may require specific permissions based on your rank. Admin commands are restricted to staff members only.

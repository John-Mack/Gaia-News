🌿 Heir of Gaia – PvP / Claim / Jobs / RPG Server

Searching for a complete, competitive, and well-balanced experience?

Welcome to Heir of Gaia, a survival PvP world where strategy, economy, and RPG progression define your journey.

⚔️ Strategic, Skill-Based PvP

Every battle matters. Optimize your gear, perfect your build, rally your allies, and take control of enemy lands. Victory isn't luck — it's preparation, coordination, and mastery.

🏰 Advanced Claim System

Secure your territory, defend your resources, and expand your influence. Whether you thrive as a solo tactician or command a dominant faction, smart planning is always rewarded.

💼 Impactful Jobs & Player-Driven Economy

Choose your path: miner, hunter, farmer, craftsman, and more. Earn income, trade with others, shape the market, and become a powerhouse within the server's economy.

🧙 Deep RPG Progression

Gain experience, unlock powerful abilities, enhance your equipment, and specialize your character to match your playstyle. The more you invest, the stronger you rise.

🌍 Stable, Competitive Environment

  • Balanced economy system
  • Frequent events
  • Active and supportive staff
  • Competitive seasonal resets
  • Advanced anti-abuse protections

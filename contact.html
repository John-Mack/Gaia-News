FAQ

How do I join the server?

Copy the server IP: amp.chaos-t.studio:5521 and add it to your Hytale server list.

Is the server free?

Yes, the server is completely free. Store purchases are optional and help support the server.

How do I report a player?

Contact a moderator on Discord with evidence (screenshots, videos) of the violation.

What's the best way to record evidence?

We recommend playing with Medal.tv installed. It lets you instantly save the last few minutes of gameplay with a single key — perfect for capturing bugs, glitches, or player violations without needing to record everything manually.

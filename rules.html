Server Rules

Welcome to Heir of Gaia! To ensure an enjoyable experience for everyone, please follow these rules.

Behavior

1

Mutual Respect

Treat all players with respect. Harassment, insults, and toxic behavior are not tolerated.

2

No Spam

Avoid spamming the chat with repetitive or useless messages.

3

Appropriate Language

Keep language appropriate for all ages. No offensive or explicit content.

Gameplay

4

No Cheating

The use of hacks, forbidden mods, or exploits is strictly prohibited.

5

No Griefing

Do not destroy other players' builds. Respect protected areas.

6

No Stealing

Do not take items that don't belong to you without permission.

Sanctions

⚠️ Warning

First minor offense

🔇 Mute

Repeated chat offenses

⏱️ Temporary Ban

Serious or repeated offenses

🚫 Permanent Ban

Very serious offenses or multiple temporary bans

Note: Administrators reserve the right to make decisions on a case-by-case basis. By playing on this server, you accept these rules.
